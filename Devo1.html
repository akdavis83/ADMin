Blog 1

Seek, Speak and Keep the Fire burning

Pressing into God, Dec 21, 2023

Seek God with all of your heart and you will find Him. Grab ahold of God's strength and peace, Isa 27:5 says that both strength and peace is freely available from Him. We find strength in His word and in His Spirit. We find peace in the closeness of His presence. Of the increase of his government and peace there shall be no end, upon the throne of David, and upon his kingdom, to order it, and to establish it with judgement and with justice from henceforth even for ever. The zeal of the Lord of hosts will perform this (Isa 9:7). Do you really want to be on fire for God? Let the zeal of the house of the Lord eat you up like that zeal did with Christ. Put on righteousness as a breastplate, salvation as a helmet, put on the garments of vengeance for clothing and zeal as a cloke. The armor of God is your spiritual warfare weapon, the shield of faith is your defense and God's word is your sword of the Spirit. Our God is an all consuming fire and it's either hot or cold, there is no neutral, lukewarm ground. When God's presence is near we can speak into that glory and see breakthrough, shifts and positive changes. Thou shalt also decree a thing, and it shall be established unto thee: and the light shall shine upon thy ways (Job 22:28). Don't just declare what you see, declare what you want to see happen in the realm of the Spirit to be established in Christ. Cultivate the breakthrough Spirit of the Breaker. Cultivate a heart posture and attitude of the Breaker living mightily within thee! Don't settle, plateau or stagnate and don't agree with evil or anything contrary to the word and do not let evil land or settle in any way, shape or form in your life. Maintain and cherish as precious every impartation of the Spirit received. Stir up the flames, keep the fire burning, stir up the gift of God and neglect not the gift that is in thee.
